CrossCraft Classic uses assets both derived and used 
from the Unity and Pixel Perfection Community Edition
resource packs.

Pixel Perfection Community Edition was used to provide
the block destroy states from terrain.png, as well as
the entirety of particles.png and particles.png. Any
other texture file is from Unity unless stated other-
wise.
